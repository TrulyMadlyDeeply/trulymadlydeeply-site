About Us

Truly Madly Deeply Ltd is a UK-based celebrations and gifting brand, specialising in products for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries and special occasions. The original website launched in 2002, and we have been trading as Truly Madly Deeply Ltd since January 2008.

Our range includes both personalised and non-personalised products, chosen for quality, presentation and the moments they help people celebrate. All purchases are currently made through Amazon for a familiar, secure checkout experience.

For personalised items, we are careful with the details. We check and confirm your personalisation, and if anything looks unclear we will contact you to confirm before it is produced.

We are based in Hersden, near Canterbury in Kent. If you have a question about an order or a personalisation option, please get in touch.

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer personalised gifts?

Yes, we offer a range of personalised gifts. On Amazon, some items include personalisation options in the listing, while others provide personalisation instructions in the product description. We check the details, and if anything is unclear we will contact you before it is produced.

How do I provide personalisation details?

If the listing does not offer personalisation fields during checkout, you can still provide details using one of these methods:

  1. During checkout, use Amazon's Gift message box to enter your personalisation. Amazon may require a "From ..." line, we normally ignore that unless you ask us to include it.
  2. After checkout, message us using Amazon's Contact seller / Message the seller option and include your personalisation.
  3. If we have not received your personalisation, we will contact you to request it before anything is produced.

Whichever method you use, we will work with you to make sure the personalisation is exactly as you want it.

How do I order?
